About Us

Center for Accounting Policy and Capital Market Research (CAPCMR) is an independent Research Organization
that works to Produce and Disseminate high quality and original research.

Our Center



What is CAPCMR?

Center for Accounting Policy and Capital Market Research was established with the objective of producing and disseminating high quality and independent research. Our research agenda is to foster thoughtful discussion on critical policy and regulatory issues pertaining to the capital market of Bangladesh.

What do we do?

At Center for Accounting Policy and Capital Market Research, we work to provide academic leadership in research, education and publication and to facilitate thoughtful and open discussions. We also help devise framework for financial regulation and accounting policy and develop solutions to challenges facing the financial sector.

In addition to our research activities, we also provide editing services to journals and academics. We also maintain the CAPCMR Market Intelligence Database, a financial resource database for listed companies in Bangladesh.

What makes us unique?

CAPCMR is a research focused enterprise, where our goal is to facilitate research on relevant financial issues with implications in the modern financial system. We ensure that every research we undertake has a material impact on our society at large, and we believe that through our work, we are helping to bridge the gap between theory and practice.

Our Services

Manuscript Services

At CAPCMR, we provide high quality manuscript editing and proofreading services and help journals, researchers, authors and academics prepare their manuscript for submission or publication. We provide field specific editing services for non-native English speakers, where we check the manuscripts for grammatical, sentence structure, word choice, sentence fragment or phrasal errors. We also help format citations and layout the manuscript to meet the specifications of the intended journal so your paper has the greatest chance of being accepted.

Your confidentiality is ensured: Our strict confidentiality and privacy policy ensures that your manuscript is safe with us.

Timely delivery: Our team of highly skilled and proficient editors can turn around your 10,000 word manuscript in 48 hours.

Available Services:

1. Manuscript Critique: Get an expert opinion on the prospects of your manuscript and get valuable advice from our editors on how to improve it.

2. Manuscript Editing: Careful editing of your draft work in various stages to fix language and grammatical issues and advice on the content.

3. Manuscript Proofreading: A final careful line-by-line review to make sure your draft is ready for submission.

4. Bundle Packaging: A combination of all the above services.

Database Services

CAPCMR develops, maintains and distributes comprehensive financial database of all listed companies in Dhaka Stock Exchange dating back to 1996.

The database contains extensive information about listed companies including the financial statements of over 3,000 firm-years of listed and delisted companies. The database is of immense usefulness to anyone involved in academic, capital market research and portfolio management. Key features of the database are: broad coverage of market, comprehensive peer-to-peer analysis opinions, industry news and event coverage and flexible options for data export.

Subscribers enjoy the ability to seamlessly access reports on companies, searching by company name or ticker symbol, or searching companies that match specific financial criteria.

Our Database Services also include:

1. Company Data: Fully searchable financial database of over 350 present and formerly listed companies on DSE and CSE.

2. Annual Reports: Fastest possible access to our collection of more than 3,000 annual reports and prospectuses.

3. Institutional Holdings: Subscribers can quickly and simply determine the institutional holders for every listed company.

4. Insider Holding: Insider holding data provides a complete, up-to-date record of all stock purchases and sales by company executives and directors.

5. FactSheet: Solution for subscribers who need presentation quality, one-page summary equity report.
